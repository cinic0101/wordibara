# Code Of Conduct

Wordibara should be a respectful and practical project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Assume contributors may have different language backgrounds and experience levels.
- Prefer clear technical reasoning over personal criticism.
- Be especially careful around kids-app privacy and safety topics.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Discriminatory comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Dismissing privacy or safety concerns without technical review

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in being blocked from participating.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository's available private channels when possible.
